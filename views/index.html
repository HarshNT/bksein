BULL KINGHT SPORTS SELECTION PROCESS

WHO CAN REGISTER?

The registration process is very simple and a nominal registration fee is charged. You can get registration with the only Rs. 599. This will give you a chance to play for the trials. These trials are held in selected districts of Gujrat.

SELECTION PROCESS

After the registration, the player will showcase his talent in the trials . After getting selected the player is enrolled for GUJRAT STATE PREMIER LEAGUE (GSPL) with the additional fees of Rs5499. The League charges entitle him to play for the inter district league in GUJRAT STATE PREMIER LEAGUE. The inter district cricket league is their ticket to auction based Grand league.

MENTORS AND EXPERTS

The talent and determination of the player will take him places. There is a team of selectors who have expertise in finding the right gem who will be the future star in the field of cricket. The experts will guide and motivate them to find their flair to play the best cricket. The mentors will give handy tips to each budding player in the selection camp.
